I am at University of Florida with a China treaty — what do I pay?
Federal: claim China Article 20 ($5,000 wage exemption) on your 1040-NR. State: nothing — Florida has no income tax. Your effective US tax bill is your federal income tax on amounts above $5,000, with Social Security and Medicare (FICA) also exempt as a nonresident F-1 student.
Does Florida have any taxes that affect international students?
The main one is sales tax on purchases (6% base rate). Florida also has a corporate income tax but that does not affect individual students. No individual income tax means no state filing obligation for F-1 or J-1 students.

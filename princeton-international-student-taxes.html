Princeton University Student Tax FAQ

Does New Jersey recognize tax treaties?
New Jersey partially recognizes some federal tax treaties. Like many states, NJ's treatment is more limited than federal. Check the NJ Division of Taxation guidance for your specific treaty country.
I live in New Jersey but my income is from Princeton — where do I file state taxes?
New Jersey — Princeton is in NJ, so your income is NJ-source. File Form NJ-1040NR as a nonresident. If you also had income from another state, you may need returns in both states.
